About Us




Our Mission

Eco Defenders is a community-driven environmental initiative that aims to tackle the growing issue of waste management in India. Our mission is to create a cleaner, greener India by empowering individuals and communities to take action against waste pollution. We believe that everyone has a role to play in protecting our planet, and by working together, we can make a significant impact.
