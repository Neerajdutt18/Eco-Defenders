Services



Our Services

Eco Defenders encourages clean, green localities by providing a comprehensive range of services. This includes offering pickup services for efficient waste management, providing certification for adopting eco-friendly practices, facilitating the uploading of blogs, images, and videos, and enabling individuals to earn through the sale of solid waste.

Waste Pickup

Efficient waste management service aimed at keeping localities clean and green. We offer hassle-free pickup solutions to responsibly dispose of waste and contribute to environmental sustainability.






Aware People

Our awareness campaigns educate and empower communities to adopt eco-friendly practices. Through informative sessions and workshops, we inspire individuals to make conscious choices for a greener future.




Get Certified

We provide certification for businesses and individuals committed to eco-friendly practices. By obtaining our certification, you demonstrate your dedication to environmental stewardship and sustainability.

Sell Products

Our platform enables individuals to earn by selling recycled or upcycled products. Join our community and turn waste into wealth while contributing to a cleaner environment.
